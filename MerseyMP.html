About Us

Merseyside Roleplay is a serious QBCore based Server. We're based off of Merseyside, UK. MerseyMP was Founded by Lukas, Grey and JT on the 12/03/2024. We're currently still in development so keep your eyes open in our Discord for updates. Feel free to suggest your ideas in our suggestion channel in Discord . We hope to be open soon so please bear with us as we're trying to bring the best experience to you!
